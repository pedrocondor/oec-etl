# Russian Sub-National Trade Data

Dataset is being provided by Customs Statistics of Foreign Trade of Russian Federation.

Product resolution : 10 digit HS classification  

Spatial resoultion : Federal Subjects  

Frequency : Monthly

Starting Year : 2015
